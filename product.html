Platform overview

Purpose-built to operationalize every obligation

Align teams on a single source of truth, automate evidence, and prove compliance continuously.

Control Library

Ingest frameworks like SOC 2, ISO 27001, and SEC rules, then map requirements to standardized controls maintained by Complyo. Version tracking highlights changes and flags required owner reviews.

  • Framework crosswalks out-of-the-box
  • Ownership and review schedules
  • Impact analysis on regulatory change

Workflow Snapshot

Assign control owners, escalate overdue actions, and auto-notify stakeholders when evidence is missing.

Evidence Automation

Integrate directly with cloud providers, HRIS, ticketing, and IAM tools. Complyo validates that collected evidence meets attestation criteria before it ever reaches an auditor.

  • No-code connectors for common systems
  • API & webhooks for custom data flows
  • Automated screenshots and attestations

Exception Routing

Trigger remedial tasks in Jira or ServiceNow with rule-based playbooks that keep teams aligned.

Audit Assurance

Maintain complete audit trails with immutable evidence history, reviewer comments, and attestation logs. Share tailored auditor portals with granular access control.

  • Pre-built auditor templates
  • Redaction controls for sensitive data
  • Export-ready reports in seconds

Outcome

Shorten audit cycles and eliminate weeklong document hunts. Deliver finished reports while competitors are still compiling spreadsheets.
