Privacy notice

How Complyo handles your data

We keep the data you share via our contact form limited, protected, and only for the reasons you expect.

What we collect

When you submit the contact form we receive the details you provide: name, email address, company, country, and the message you send.

Why we use it

We process these details to respond to demo or beta requests and other inbound inquiries. This is done on the basis of legitimate interest and pre-contractual steps for B2B engagements.

How it is processed

Form submissions are routed through Formspree, our form processing sub-processor. Formspree provides secure delivery and routing of your message to the Complyo team.

Where data lives

Complyo intends to host and process inquiry data within the EU. Formspree may process data in other regions to provide their service. You can review their Data Processing Addendum for more info.

How long we retain it

We keep inquiry details until the conversation is resolved or until you ask us to delete them.

Your rights

You may request access, corrections, or deletion at any time by contacting hello@complyo.eu. We will respond promptly to all requests.
